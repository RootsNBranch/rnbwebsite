Roots N Branch Siva Academy

At RNB, we blend traditional Samoan dance forms like Siva and Fa’ataupati (Roots) with contemporary dance styles (Branch). Our choreography mixes vibrant modern beats with timeless Samoan songs—keeping culture alive, relevant, and powerful for every generation.

What We Offer

Workshops & Classes

Learn foundational Siva and Fa’ataupati, as well as contemporary dance fusion.

Performances

We bring energy to the stage with a fusion of tradition and today’s rhythm.

Cultural Education

Engage in the deeper meaning of Samoan movement and storytelling.
