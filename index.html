Roots N Branch Siva Academy

Bridging generations through Samoan tradition and modern movement

Our Story

At RNB, we blend traditional Samoan dance forms like Siva and Fa’ataupati (Roots) with contemporary dance styles (Branch). Our choreography mixes vibrant modern beats with timeless Samoan songs—keeping culture alive, relevant, and powerful for every generation.

What We Offer

Workshops & Classes

Learn foundational Siva and Fa’ataupati, as well as contemporary dance fusion.

Performances

We bring energy to the stage with a fusion of tradition and today’s rhythm.

Cultural Education

Engage in the deeper meaning of Samoan movement and storytelling.

🔥 Class Structure 🔥

👦🏽 Kids Siva (Ages 5–10)

Introductory class to Samoan Siva—focusing on rhythm, movement, and storytelling. Builds confidence, coordination, and cultural identity.

🧑🏽‍🦱 Teen to Adult (Males Only)

Traditional Siva & Fa’ataupati training for young men. Focus on performance, strength, and cultural discipline rooted in heritage.

🎶 Siva X Hip Hop

A modern fusion class combining the power of Samoan Siva with hip hop. Bold choreography to blend tradition with today’s beats. Open to all genders.

💪🏽 Siva Fit

Movement-based fitness inspired by traditional Fa’ataupati and Siva. High energy, cultural grounding, all fitness levels welcome.

🌟 RNB Showcase Crew

Elite performance crew representing the RNB Academy. For experienced dancers ready to perform and innovate with cultural pride.
