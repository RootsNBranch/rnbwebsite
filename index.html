🔥 Roots N Branch Siva Academy – Class Structure 🔥

👦🏽 Kids Siva (Ages 5–10)

A fun intro to Samoan Siva, rhythm, and storytelling. Builds confidence, coordination, and cultural pride.

🧑🏽‍🦱 Teen to Adult Siva (Males Only)

Traditional Siva & Fa’ataupati classes for young men focused on strength, cultural discipline, and performance skills.

🎶 Siva X Hip Hop (Teen to Adult)

Fusion of Siva with hip hop and modern movement. Choreography to remixes and beats that blend past and present. Open to all genders.

💪🏽 Siva Fit

Cardio-based movement inspired by traditional Siva and Fa’ataupati. Boost stamina, build strength, and stay rooted in culture. Open to all levels.

🌟 RNB Showcase Crew (Invite Only)

Advanced performance team. For dancers ready to represent at events and competitions. Focused on precision, storytelling, and cultural leadership.

Our Story

At RNB, we blend traditional Samoan dance forms like Siva and Fa’ataupati (Roots) with contemporary dance styles (Branch). Our choreography mixes vibrant modern beats with timeless Samoan songs—keeping culture alive, relevant, and powerful for every generation.

What We Offer

Workshops & Classes

Learn foundational Siva and Fa’ataupati, as well as contemporary dance fusion.

Performances

We bring energy to the stage with a fusion of tradition and today’s rhythm.

Cultural Education

Engage in the deeper meaning of Samoan movement and storytelling.
