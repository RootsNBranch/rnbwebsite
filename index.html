Bridging generations through Samoan tradition and modern movement

Our Story

At RNB, we blend traditional Samoan dance forms like Siva and Fa’ataupati (Roots) with contemporary dance styles (Branch). Our choreography mixes vibrant modern beats with timeless Samoan songs—keeping culture alive, relevant, and powerful for every generation.

🔥 Class Offerings 🔥

👦🏽 Kids Siva (Ages 5–10)

A fun and foundational class focusing on rhythm, confidence, and Samoan cultural storytelling for tamaiti.

🧑🏽‍🦱 Teen to Adult Siva (Males Only)

Learn Fa’ataupati and traditional Siva for young men, focusing on discipline, strength, and performance-based culture.

🎶 Siva X Hip Hop

A high-energy fusion class mixing traditional moves with modern choreography. Open to all genders.

💪🏽 Siva Fit

Cardio + Culture. A fitness class rooted in Siva and Fa’ataupati movements for strength, stamina, and wellness.

🌟 RNB Showcase Crew

Train and perform with our elite team—representing RNB through dynamic cultural fusion on stage.
